Explore Kollam

Kollam is a historic port city in Kerala, India, that offers a blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and modern amenities. You can admire the scenic Ashtamudi lake, the second largest in Kerala, and enjoy boating, fishing, or birdwatching. You can also visit the ancient Karunagappally Mosque, the elegant Thevally Palace, and the spiritual Amritapuri ashram of the humanitarian leader Amma. Kollam is also known for its cashew industry, and you can taste the delicious nuts and buy them as souvenirs. If you are looking for some adventure, you can head to the Adventure Park, where you can try various rides and games.
