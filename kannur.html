Explore Kannur

Kannur, also known as Cannanore, is a coastal city in Kerala that offers a rich blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. You can explore the ancient forts, temples, and museums that showcase the legacy of the Kolathiri rajas, the Portuguese, and the British. You can also witness the vibrant art form of Theyyam, a ritual dance that invokes the divine. Kannur is blessed with scenic beaches, such as Payyambalam, Thottada, and Mappila Bay, where you can relax, enjoy the sunset, and indulge in water sports. Kannur is a perfect destination for those who love to discover the spice of life.
